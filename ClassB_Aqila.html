A little something to try 📚
You've got the ideas and the confidence — now it's just about giving your words a little more colour. Think of vocabulary like a wardrobe: the same pieces always work, but imagine having a few more beautiful options to reach for.
  • Notice collocations — words that live together naturally. 'Make a decision', 'take a risk', 'raise a concern'. They're everywhere once you start looking.
  • When you find a word you love, write it down with its sentence — context helps it stick far better than a definition alone.
  • Try swapping one safe word per session. Instead of 'very important', maybe 'crucial'. Just one swap — see how it feels!
